## 3. Behavior contract: what `_extract_ledger_check_integers` strips

After this PR, the function strips text in the following order (this is also the docstring you must write):

| # | Pattern | Source phrase example | Why strip |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `\d{1,3}(?:,\d{3})+` | `8,400 km` | thousand separator → join digits |
| 2 | `\d{4}-\d{2}-\d{2}` | `2026-05-13` | ISO date |
| 3 | `\d{1,2}/\d{1,2}` | `5/13` | slash date |
| 4 | `\d+月\d+日` | `5月13日` | Chinese date |
| 5 | `\d{4}年` | `2026年` | year reference |
| 6 | `HPI\s*指数[^。；\n]{0,40}（当前\s*\d+）` | `HPI 指数持平（当前 24）` | HPI parenthetical (strict closing) |
| 7 | `HPI\s*指数[^。；\n]{0,20}\d+` | `HPI 指数持平（当前 24，一般关注）` | HPI parenthetical (lenient fallback) |
| 8 | `\bHPI\s+\d+\b` | `HPI 24` | bare HPI mention |
| 9 | `（当前\s*\d+）` | `（当前 24）` | standalone parenthetical (orphan) |
| 10 | `\d+\s*天前` | `13 天前` | lag suffix |
| **11 (NEW)** | `至今\s*\d+\s*天` | `至今 13 天` / `至今13天` | **lag prefix (this PR)** |
| 12 | `距[^。；\n]{0,40}\d+\s*天` | `距 WHO 官方更新 13 天` | distance lag clause |
| 13 | `近\s*\d+\s*(?:小时|h|H)\b` | `近 24 小时` | recent-hours window |

Order matters: thousand-separator strip (1) must run before any digit-token regex; the strict HPI regex (6) runs before the lenient fallback (7).

## 9. Out-of-band notes for cursor

- The `\s*` between `至今` and the digit is intentional. Real LLM output has both `至今13天` (no space) and `至今 13 天` (single space) variants.
- Do **not** generalize to `\d+\s*天` (without `至今` anchor). That would over-strip phrases like `7 天内累计 N 例` where the `N` is a real allowed count.
